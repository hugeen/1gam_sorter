Welcome to 1GAM Sorter Javascript BookMark

Tired of having to scroll down to find a game that interest you ? I have the solution!

I coded a small javascript bookmark that will allow you to filter the results by tags

All you have to do is drag and drop this link to your bookmarks bar in your browser : 1GAM Sorter

Then you go to the games page of One Game A Month. Then you click the bookmark when the page is fully loaded. (Wait few seconds until processing)

Have fun !

Welcome to 1GAM Sorter Javascript BookMark

Update: V2 - 2013-03-22 - Updated for the new 1GAM website version & month filter added!

Tired of having to scroll down to find a game that interest you ? I have the solution!

I coded a small javascript bookmarklet that will allow you to filter the results by tags

All you have to do is drag and drop this link to your bookmarks bar in your browser : 1GAM Sorter

Then you go to the games list page of One Game A Month. Then you click the bookmark when the page is fully loaded and wait few seconds until processing

Have fun !
